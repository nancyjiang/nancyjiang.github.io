Summary

Nancy graduated from the University of California, Berkeley, where she studied Economics. Upon graduation, she joined the PFM Group as an Analyst in the San Francisco office where she completed transactions for bond issuances and other fixed-income related deals for municipal clients. After working at PFM for 3.5 years, she enrolled as an MBA student at the Kellogg School of Management in Evanston, IL where she is currently majoring in Finance and Management and Strategy. This past summer, she interned as an Investment Banking Summer Associate at J.P. Morgan in the Technology, Media and Telecom group in San Francisco.
